About Roberta Schultz

Hi, I'm Roberta Schultz, a full-time visual designer living in Toronto, Canada. My strong suits are editorial design and branding. I must, however, admit I'm quite smitten with user experience design and creative programming.

I have a Master of Arts in Contemporary Art, Design & New Medias Art Histories from OCAD University, which explains my passion for contemporary art and research. I speak four languages and have a mixed background of design, teaching and museum experience.

I have an unusual sense of humor, a modernist approach to design and a very insightful type of personality. Just the creative kind you've been seeking!
